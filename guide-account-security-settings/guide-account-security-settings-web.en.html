Account Security Settings

Register & Login

Sign up with your Email or Phone Number, and set a strong password.

When logging in, avoid public Wi-Fi or insecure devices to reduce the risk of account compromise.

⚠️ Note: Anyone asking for your password or verification code is likely a scammer. Do not share them.

KYC Verification

To ensure fund security and regulatory compliance, please complete KYC (Identity Verification).

Once verified, you can increase withdrawal limits and unlock more features.

Your identity information is kept confidential and used solely for compliance and security purposes.

2FA Authentication

We recommend enabling 2FA, such as Google Authenticator, SMS, or Email codes.

This adds an extra layer of protection during login, withdrawals, or setting changes, ensuring only you access the account.

Fund Password

Please set a Fund Password in security settings.

This password is required for withdrawals, transfers, or high-risk actions, preventing unauthorized fund transfers even if your account is compromised.

Login History

You can view recent login times, locations, and IP addresses in "Account Activity".

If you spot unusual activity (e.g., unknown location or device), change your password immediately and contact support.

Anti-Phishing Tips

Please ensure you are on the Official Website. Beware of phishing sites or emails asking for passwords.

⚠️ Warning: Official support will NEVER ask for your password, private keys, or transfers to personal wallets.
